# Knoxville R Users Group <img style="padding: 14px" align="right" src="ui/img/landing.jpeg" width=375 title="Quote from Barbie, math from Baker."> We're a community interested in sharing our experiences with the R programming language. Anyone is free to join, and members are encouraged to give presentations about basically anything you want, so long as it's related to R. Share your experiences, frustrations, and insights with a community of like-minded professionals. We organize our meetings on [Meetup](http://www.meetup.com/Knoxville-R-Users-Group/). If you want to attend, all you have to do is show up; but we prefer if you RSVP on meetup. If you're interested in organizing a meeting, feel free to get in touch with any of the group organizers.
